## Random Variables

$$ E(AX + BY + C) = A \cdot E(X) + B \cdot E(Y) + C $$
$$ Var(AX + BY) = A^{2} \cdot Var(X) + B^{2} \cdot Var(Y) + 2AB \cdot Cov(X, Y) \qquad \small\text{( Cov(X, Y) = 0 if X and Y are independent)} $$
